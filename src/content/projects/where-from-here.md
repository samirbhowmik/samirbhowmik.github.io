*Where From Here* is a short performance film made with New York artist Lenore
Malen, questioning the paradoxical nature of freedom in times of quarantine and
virtual isolation. A remote correspondence between Hudson, New York and Helsinki,
it takes the form of video performances and readings — drawing on a 1989
conversation between Theodor Adorno and Ernst Bloch on attainable possibilities.
