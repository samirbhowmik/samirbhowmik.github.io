Lost Islands takes you on a performative journey through the wilderness and
ruins of Vallisaari, where the repressed ghosts of technology and infrastructure
search for utopia. A commentary on the oppression of the human in the
post-Anthropocene, it traces the conquest of nature and the labouring body by
colonialism for resource extraction, and the capture of bodies into modern
infrastructure and, ultimately, AI-driven automation.

Commissioned by the Helsinki Biennial 2021. Project advised by Jussi Parikka.
