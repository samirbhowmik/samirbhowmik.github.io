Heat Work (2022–24) investigates the energy and heat of movement data generated
by tribal dances from an extraction zone. The methodology combines motion
capture, data classification, and a supervised machine-learning model, asking
how a sustainable approach to choreography with AI might be possible.

Commissioned by the Just-AI project of the London School of Economics, funded by
the AHRC (UK) and the Ada Lovelace Foundation. Part of the Terra-Performing research project.
