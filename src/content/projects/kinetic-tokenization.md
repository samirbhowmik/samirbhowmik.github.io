*"Intelligence begins with ethical motion."*

Kinethic confronts a question raised across recent work: when a dancer's
movement is captured and used to train generative models, who owns the result?
The platform treats motion not as an extractable resource but as authored
cultural knowledge — embedding informed consent, attribution, and royalty
distribution into the data itself through blockchain provenance.

The pipeline moves from ethical data collection through computer-vision movement
analysis, to discrete movement tokens, to smart contracts that automate
royalties and verify consent for every downstream use across gaming, robotics,
film, and healthcare.

Kinethic is both an artwork and a working prototype — a counter-proposal to the
extractive logics that govern motion economies, and a companion to the
*Kinethics* and *Resisting Capture* strands of the Terra-Performing research.
