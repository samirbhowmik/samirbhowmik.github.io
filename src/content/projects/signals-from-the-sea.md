Signals from the Sea is part of the *Lost Islands* project at the Helsinki
Biennial 2021. Combining 3D animation, electronic sound, vocals, and artificial
intelligence, the installation deploys machine learning on an archive of
submarine cables and marine biological lifeforms to unveil a non-human entity
spawned from the crumbling infrastructures of the so-called Anthropocene. A
virtual-gallery edition was presented in ARILYN (2022).
