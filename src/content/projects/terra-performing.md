Terra-Performing is a multidisciplinary research project that investigates
extractivism through intelligent performance methods. Extractivism driven by
automation and AI threatens to automate environmental damage and accelerate
climate change; the project engages this discourse through three
interdisciplinary studies examining inclusivity, sustainability, and
participation in intelligent performance. Its objective is a sustainable
framework that incorporates global performative traditions, energy, and
environmental impact into artistic practice — spanning Critical AI, Environmental
Media, and Performance Studies.

The artworks **Heat Work**, **Choreographies of Discard**, and **Lost Islands**
are produced within this project, alongside the **Kinethic** tokenization platform.
