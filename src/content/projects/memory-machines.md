*Memory Machines* explores the infrastructure of the Oodi Library in Helsinki,
combining dance, theatre and circus with a guided tour through the building's
concealed infrastructural sites — from climate control to power management, from
checkout terminals to data centres. How do the flows of data, heat, matter and
energy sustain cultural memory?

Realised as part of *The Library's Other Intelligences*, organised by the Finnish
Cultural Institute in New York (FCINY) through its MOBIUS Fellowship with the
Helsinki Public Library, curated by Shannon Mattern and Jussi Parikka. Supported
by the Kone Foundation.
