Choreographies of Discard is an extractive enquiry, juxtaposing the labour of
dance and the work of machines — both physical and computational — into a
critical narrative. Background machine learning and data analysis is performed
on *mudras*, the gestural movements of Bharatanatyam, a classical Indian dance.
Part of the Terra-Performing research project.
